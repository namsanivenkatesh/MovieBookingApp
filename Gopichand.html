About

Gopichand is a Telugu actor and the son of filmmaker T. Krishna. He is known for his work in films like Nijam (2003), Okkadunnadu (2007) and Sahasam (2013). Gopichand procured a degree in Engineering from Russia, after which, he decided to join the film industry. He made his debut in the Telugu movie, Tholi Valapu (2001). He went on to play the role of the antagonist in his next movie, Jayam (2002). Gopichand subsequently appeared in Yagnam (2004), Lakshyam (2007), Wanted (2011), Loukyam (2014). His 2017 releases include Telugu movies like Oxygen, Goutham Nanda, and Aaradugula Bullet. His other releases include Pantham (2018) and Chanakya (2019). Gopichand`s 2021 release includes Seetimaarr.
